What is Cafe Column?

Hi! We are Hailey and Shams. We are two college students who share a hobby for trying new cafes and are always on the hunt for the best cafes to study in. We created this site for cafe hoppers and coffee lovers alike. We hope to make cafe visits a more interactive experience with friends where you can share your favorite cafe spots and find your new favorite study spot!
